OPERATION

Put the Incubator Oven into Operation

Perform the following steps and procedures to put the unit into operation after installing it in a new workspace environment.

 

  1. Plug in the power cord.

    2. Attach the power cord that came with the unit to the inlet receptacle on the left side of the incubator.

    Plug the power cord into the workspace electrical outlet.

  2. Turn on the incubator oven.

    3. Place the incubator Power Switch in the ON ( I ) position.

     

     

  3. Verify Vacuum Integrity.

    4. Evacuate the oven chamber and hold under vacuum for 10 minutes to verify the integrity of the vacuum supply system.

     

  4. Set the Operating Temperature.

    5. Set the constant temperature set point.

  5. Set the Over Temperature Limit.

The incubator must be heated and stable at your application temperature to perform this procedure.

 

The incubator oven is now ready for use.

 

End of procedure
