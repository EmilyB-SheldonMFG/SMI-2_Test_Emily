Exporting the Data Log

The incubator exports user-specified date ranges from the log as spreadsheet-readable .CSV files.

 

  1. Insert a USB A thumb drive in the incubator USB Port.
    • The port is located on the right side of the unit.
    • Note: The Export page cannot be accessed unless a thumb drive is in the port.
  2. In the Settings menu, tap the Export to USB button.
  3. Enter a file name and tap Enter.
  4. Enter a date range, "From" and "Until". This is the date range for the incubator exports.
    • Saving the Until value starts the download.

Time Stamp (Column D)

The incubator records time in seconds using a UNIX time stamp (Epoch).
