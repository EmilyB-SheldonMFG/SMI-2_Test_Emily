Installation Cleaning and Disinfection

The manufacturer recommends cleaning the shelving and unit chamber prior to installation of the shelving in the chamber.

    The unit was cleaned at the factory but may have been exposed to contaminants during shipping.

    Remove all wrappings and coverings from shelving prior to cleaning and installation. Do not clean the shelving with deionized water.

    Please see the Cleaning and Disinfection procedure on page 1 in the User Maintenance chapter for information on how to clean and disinfect without damaging the unit.
