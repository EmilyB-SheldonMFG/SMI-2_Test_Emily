*The SMI12-2 comes with two power cords. It requires 4.0 amps for each cord.

    Supplied voltage must not vary more than 10% from the data plate rating. Damage to the unit may result if supplied voltage varies more than 10%.

    The wall power source must be protective earth grounded.

    Use a separate circuit to prevent loss of the unit due to overloading or circuit failure.

    The recommended wall circuit breakers for these units are 16 amps.

    The wall power source must conform to all national and local electrical codes.
